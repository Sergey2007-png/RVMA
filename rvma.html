Problem Solving

What problems does creating a website solve?

Lack of Online Visibility

Without a website, you miss out on potential customers. A professional website helps you establish an online presence and attract new clients.

Lack of Customer Trust

Customers trust companies with professional websites. A website boosts trust in your brand by providing all necessary information about your services and products.

Limited Marketing Opportunities

A website provides a platform for effective marketing campaigns, allowing you to attract more customers and increase sales.

Development stages

Business Analysis and Strategy

The first step is to analyze the niche, competitors and target audience. This helps to determine the structure of the site and its functionality, taking into account the needs of users and the characteristics of the market. The data obtained forms the basis of the product development strategy.

Prototype Development

Before creating the interface, we develop a prototype of the project, experimenting with the arrangement of blocks and elements. We cooperate with the client, coordinating ideas and taking into account wishes. The sketch should convey the general structure, logic and functionality.

Site Design

We develop UX/UI for the site - creating an interface based on a prototype. The UI developer works on the visual style, while the UX developer optimizes user experience, improving convenience and interaction speed.

Website Programming

Programming begins with Frontend development, focusing on design implementation, loading speed, and responsiveness. Backend development enhances the site's internal workings, ensuring quality, performance, and security.

Testing

Website testing ensures functionality and usability are thoroughly checked before launch. It helps identify errors and shortcomings, allowing corrections before the site goes live. Testing includes both automated and manual methods, plus specialized software.

Deployment and Support

After project completion, we publish and test the site for accuracy and speed. We set up analytics for monitoring and provide one month of free technical support for changes and adjustments.
